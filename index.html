About Mabirizi Emmanuel

Committed to Serving Busiro East

Mabirizi Emmanuel is a dedicated leader with a vision to transform Busiro East into a model constituency. With a background in community development and youth empowerment, Emmanuel brings fresh ideas and energy to the political landscape.

His campaign focuses on education, healthcare, infrastructure development, and economic empowerment for all residents of Busiro East. Emmanuel believes in transparent, accountable leadership that puts the people first.

As a Master Guide in the Seventh-day Adventist Church, Emmanuel's leadership is rooted in strong moral values and service to humanity. His motto "Kulwa Busiro Empya" reflects his commitment to creating a new, better Busiro East for all.

Construction of wells

The impact of a single well ripples far beyond its immediate surroundings. Children spend less time fetching water and more time in school, families enjoy better health, and entire villages gain resilience against droughts. By investing in well construction, we’re not just digging holes in the ground—we’re laying the foundation for stronger, healthier, and more self-sufficient communities. Every well represents hope, dignity, and a sustainable future for generations to come.
